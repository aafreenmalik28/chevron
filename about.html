About us

14

Years of
Experience

Delivering Quality Solutions

Chevron Pharma Pvt. Ltd., established in 2010 and located in Ankleshwar, India, is a leading manufacturer and exporter of high-quality Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs) and drug intermediates. With over a decade of experience, we have earned the trust of clients worldwide for our commitment to innovation, regulatory compliance, and superior manufacturing standards. We specialize in APIs like Lidocaine, Prilocaine, and their respective hydrochlorides, serving both domestic and international markets.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities follow strict cGMP (current Good Manufacturing Practices) guidelines and are regularly inspected by FDA and other global regulatory bodies. These advanced facilities allow us to produce APIs in various batch sizes while ensuring consistent quality. Equipped with modern analytical and microbiological labs, our dedicated team ensures every product meets global pharmaceutical standards.

At Chevron Pharma Pvt. Ltd., we prioritize sustainability and employee safety. We integrate eco-friendly practices into our operations and maintain a safe work environment with regular health check-ups and training. We remain focused on continuously improving our processes to meet the evolving needs of the pharmaceutical industry while ensuring patient safety and customer satisfaction.
