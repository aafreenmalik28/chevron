Lidocaine Hydrochloride

CAS No.: 6108-05-0
Grades: IP/BP/EP/USP

A water-soluble salt form of lidocaine, widely used as a local anesthetic and antiarrhythmic agent. It is commonly used in injections, topical solutions, and gels for numbing tissues before surgical or dental procedures.

Prilocaine Hydrochloride

CAS No.: 1786-81-8
Grades: IP/BP/EP/USP

A hydrochloride salt of prilocaine, known for its excellent local anesthetic properties with reduced systemic toxicity. It is used in both dental and medical procedures, often combined with other agents for enhanced anesthetic effects.
