About Us

Delivering Quality Solutions

Chevron Pharma Pvt. Ltd. is a trusted leader in the manufacturing and export of Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs) and drug intermediates.

Since 2010, we have been committed to delivering high-quality, reliable products to global markets. Our advanced manufacturing facilities adhere to stringent cGMP standards and are regularly inspected by FDA and other regulatory authorities to ensure compliance. We focus on continuous innovation and excellence to meet the growing needs of the pharmaceutical industry, all while maintaining a strong commitment to sustainability, safety, and customer satisfaction.

  • High-Quality Pharmaceutical Production
  • Strict Compliance with cGMP Standards
  • Sustainable and Safe Manufacturing Practices
  • Innovative Solutions for Healthcare Professionals

Sustainability

Strong corporate commitment to safety, health, and environment

  • Basic safety rules & procedures: Establishing foundational safety measures and protocols to ensure a safe working environment for all employees.
  • Material Safety Data Sheet (MSDS): A document that provides detailed information about hazardous materials, their handling, storage, and safety measures in case of an emergency.
  • Regular safety training: Continuous safety education for employees to keep them informed of the latest safety procedures and equipment usage.
  • Safety audits, inspections: Periodic checks and evaluations to ensure safety regulations are followed and identify potential hazards or areas of improvement.
  • On-site emergency management plan: A comprehensive strategy outlining actions to take in case of an emergency, including evacuation procedures and designated safety officers.
  • Personnel Protective Equipment: The use of safety gear such as helmets, gloves, goggles, and clothing to protect employees from workplace hazards.
  • Work Permit Procedure: A formal process ensuring that work activities, especially those involving hazardous conditions, are safely planned, authorized, and monitored.
  • Periodic Medical Examination: Regular health checks to monitor employees' physical condition, ensuring they are fit for their work and identifying any health risks early.
