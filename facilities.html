State-of-the-Art Manufacturing Facility

Chevron Pharma Pvt. Ltd. boasts a multipurpose, fully integrated manufacturing facility equipped with cutting-edge technology to ensure the seamless production of Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs) and intermediates. Our facility is designed to handle production scales ranging from a few kilograms to several tons while adhering strictly to cGMP guidelines.

Regularly inspected by the FDA and other global regulatory authorities, our advanced infrastructure includes three dedicated powder processing lines, all operating within Class-8 cleanroom environments. This ensures the highest standards of quality, safety, and regulatory compliance, reinforcing our commitment to delivering superior pharmaceutical products.

Quality Control & Assurance

At Chevron Pharma Pvt. Ltd., quality is the cornerstone of our operations. We are dedicated to maintaining the highest quality standards through a well-equipped analytical laboratory and microbiological laboratory, ensuring that every API we produce meets stringent global regulations. Our APIs are specified and analyzed according to relevant pharmacopoeias and in-house specifications, benchmarked against reference standards for precision and consistency. We strictly adhere to cGMP guidelines, ensuring that all raw materials, intermediates, finished products, auxiliary materials, and packaging materials comply with regulatory requirements.

The foundation of high-quality pharmaceutical products lies in the careful selection of raw materials, controlled production conditions, and cutting-edge technology. Our experienced analytical development team continuously develops and validates advanced analytical methods and specifications, guaranteeing product safety, efficacy, and consistency at every stage of manufacturing.

Effluent Treatment Plant (ETP)

At Chevron Pharma Pvt. Ltd., we recognize the importance of sustainable and environmentally responsible manufacturing. Our state-of-the-art Effluent Treatment Plant (ETP) is designed to efficiently manage and treat effluents generated from our multi-product manufacturing facility.

Our advanced ETP infrastructure includes:

  • ✅ 100 KL Primary Treatment Capacity – Ensuring effective initial treatment of effluents.
  • ✅ 300 KL Secondary Treatment Capacity – Utilizing cutting-edge treatment processes for enhanced purification.
  • ✅ 300 KL Storage Capacity – Ensuring safe handling and controlled discharge of treated water.

By implementing stringent environmental controls and adhering to regulatory compliance, we ensure that our operations are sustainable, eco-friendly, and aligned with global environmental standards. Our commitment to green manufacturing helps us minimize environmental impact while maintaining operational excellence.
